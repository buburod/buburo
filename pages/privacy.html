Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024-09-01
At Buburo, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you interact with our products and services, including Webink. This Privacy and Cookies Policy outlines the practices of Buburo related to the collection, use, sharing, and protection of your personal data, as well as your rights as a data subject.

1. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy and Cookies Policy applies to all products and services offered by Buburo, including Webink, and to the personal data we collect when you interact with these products. It also governs the offline services that support our products. This policy does not apply to third-party websites or services that are linked to or integrated with our products but operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

2. Information We Collect

We collect various types of personal information to provide and improve our products and services. The information we collect may include:

a) Information You Provide
When you use Webink or other Buburo products, you may be required to create an account or log in via social login (e.g., Google). During this process, we collect:
• Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, and other details provided by Google or other third-party authentication services.
• Manga Library Data: Data regarding your manga preferences, saved titles, and chapter progress, which is stored locally on your device or in your Google account for synchronization purposes.

b) Information Collected Automatically
When you interact with our products, we collect data automatically through cookies and other tracking technologies. This may include:
• Device Information: Information about the device you are using, such as your device’s manufacturer and model.
• Usage Data: Information about how you use our products, such as IP addresses, browser type, pages viewed, and the actions taken within our application.
c) Data from Third-Party Sources
We may also receive additional information about you from other sources, such as demographic data, which can be combined with the data we collect directly.

3. How We Use Your Information

Buburo uses your personal information for the following purposes:

a) To Provide and Improve Our Services
• User Experience: We use your information to personalize your experience, such as synchronizing your manga library across devices.
• Security: Your information helps us maintain and improve the security of our products by preventing unauthorized access and ensuring a safe user environment.


b) To Communicate with You
We may use your information to:
• Send you updates: Regarding your account, security notifications, or other important service-related information.
• Marketing and promotions: Where permitted by law, we may send you promotional materials about new products or features. You can opt out of these communications at any time.

c) To Comply with Legal Obligations
We may process your personal data to comply with applicable legal obligations, respond to legal processes, and enforce our terms and conditions.

4. Data Storage and Security

a) Data Storage
Your personal data, including your manga library and preferences, is stored:
• Locally on your device: For easy access and usage.
• In your Google account: If you choose to sync your data across devices.

b) Data Security
We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include encryption, secure data storage, and access controls.
However, no system is completely secure. Therefore, while we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

a) What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files placed on your device that help us enhance your experience with our products. We use cookies and similar technologies to:
• Store Preferences: Remember your settings, such as preferred language and saved manga.
• Analyze Usage: Track how you interact with our products to improve functionality and user experience.

b) Managing Cookies
You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings. However, if you disable cookies, certain features of Webink or other Buburo products may not function as intended.
For more detailed information about managing cookies, visit www.allaboutcookies.org.

6. Sharing Your Information

Buburo does not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share your information in the following circumstances:

a) With Your Consent
We will share your information with third parties only when you have provided explicit consent for specific uses.

b) Service Providers
We may share personal data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our products, providing support, or improving services. These service providers are bound by contractual agreements to maintain the confidentiality and security of your personal data.

c) Legal Compliance and Protection
We may disclose your personal information if required by law or to protect Buburo’s rights, property, or safety, or that of our users. This may include complying with legal processes, responding to requests from government authorities, or enforcing our terms and conditions.

7. International Data Transfers

As part of providing our services, your personal data may be transferred to and stored in countries outside of your country of residence. Buburo ensures that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in compliance with applicable data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users within the European Union.

8. Your Rights

You have the following rights concerning your personal data:
• Access: You can request access to the personal data we hold about you.
• Correction: You can request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete data.
• Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data in certain circumstances.
• Portability: You can request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
• Withdraw Consent: You can withdraw consent to data processing where consent was the basis for collection.
If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.

9. Retention of Data

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide the services you have requested, or for other essential purposes such as complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements. When your data is no longer necessary, it will be securely deleted or anonymized.

10. Changes to the Privacy and Cookies Policy

Buburo reserves the right to modify this Privacy and Cookies Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted within the application or on our website. Your continued use of our products after the posting of any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
We recommend that you review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.
